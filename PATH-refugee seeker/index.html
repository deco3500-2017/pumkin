Welcome to PATH

Path is a web application provide a social community that makes connections between refugees and aid organizations. By using this, refugees and asylum seekers may find themselves in a dilemma in their countries of asylum. Moreover, Path also creates an solution for refugees and asylum seekers who have language and communication barriers to locate and contact different types of supporting community in Australia.
